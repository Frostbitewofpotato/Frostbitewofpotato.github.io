Recipe

  1. keep the acids (nitric acid and sulfuric acid) at about 72 degree F
  2. ensure that there is an ice cold pool of water nearby incase something goes wrong
  3. mix a 1 to 1 solution of nitric acid and sulfuric acid
  4. SLOWELY add the glycerin to the mixed acid
  5. and thats it!

Safety

  • make sure the area is well ventalated and/or people where gas masks.
  • if the temperature of the mixed acid ever exeeds 86 degree f then imediatly drown it in the cold water
  • if the Glycerin is added to quickly then a toxic and explosive gas will be realeased, evacuate imediatly
