Cheese Glazed Chicken

Intro

a recipe created... I think by my grandmother? she's the only one who knows how to make it perfectly so I assume it's hers anyway. It's truely delicioiuse, this is a rreal recipe unlike the other one so I don't have any notes to share

from my mother, AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA

Ingrediant amount
Skinned Chicken Breasts 6 pieces
Flour 1-2 T
Paprika 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Butter 2T
oil 1T
Sherry 1/4c
Cornstarch 1 tsp
Light Cream 3/4 c
White Wine 1/3 c
Lemon Juice 1 T
Swiss Cheese 1/2 C

Instructions

  1. Place chicken breasts in plastic bag with flour, paprika, and salt. Shake until coated
  2. Brown the pieces of chicken with butter and oil over moderate heat
  3. Add sherrt and cook untill the chicken is tender, about 20-30 minutes
  4. before making the sauce, move the chicken to a platter
  5. Blend the cornstarch with light cream. Stir into pan droppings and continue to stir until sauce thickens
  6. add the white wine and lemon juice, then heat for a few more minutes
  8. sprinkle on the cheese and then let sit for 5 min
  9. serve
